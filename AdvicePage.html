Advice

A proposed graduation plan sheet outlines the remaining courses you will need to complete in your final semesters upon graduation. To ensure that you are on the right track, and you won't need any extra classes than already anticipated, aim to complete your graduation plan sheet at the end of your junior year. Send your graduation plan to your CECS advisor so they can sign off on it. Download the graduation sheet below.
