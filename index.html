Advice Page

A proposed graduation plan sheet outlines the remaining courses you will need to complete in your final semesters upon graduation. To ensure that you are on the right track, and you won't need any extra classes than already anticipated, aim to complete your graduation plan sheet at the end of your junior year. Send your graduation plan sheet to your CECS advisor so they can sign off on it. For your reference, a graduation plan sheet below is available for download.

To ensure a spot in the commencement program, you must also complete a Degree/Diploma Application by the beginning of the term that you expect to complete your degree requirements. An online application can be accessed here. To fill out the application by hand, download the form below.
